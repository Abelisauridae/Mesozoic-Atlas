# Sample Family Prompts

These are first-pass production prompts for the approved illustrated-card direction.

## Abelisauridae

```text
Create a square illustrated dinosaur family card for Abelisauridae, a dinosaur family from the Dinosaur Atlas project. Show one featured adult abelisaurid as the clear focal subject using a giant-theropod archetype: head-and-torso dominant, powerful forward stride, strong 3/4 view, expressive jaws, and heavy silhouette impact. Emphasize the family-defining anatomy: deep short skull, horned or ornamented brow region, tiny forelimbs, powerful hind legs, muscular tail, compact predatory build. The animal must read instantly at thumbnail size.

Visual style: premium natural-history illustration card, crisp black linework, soft cel-shaded color, subtle grain texture, muted realistic palette, museum-compendium aesthetic, simplified but believable prehistoric habitat, polished digital illustration rather than photoreal painting.

Environment: semi-arid volcanic highland with rocky ground, sparse desert plants, and distant smoking cones kept simple behind the silhouette. Lighting: warm sunset or late-afternoon sky light with clean shadow shapes. Color direction: dusty taupe, charcoal, warm ember orange, muted mauve sky, and dry earth brown. Mood: fierce, iconic, and controlled rather than monstrous.

Layout constraints: square composition, rounded card border area, reserved blank title band at the bottom for later typography, optional reserved badge space in the lower-right corner, no rendered text, no logo, no watermark.
```
